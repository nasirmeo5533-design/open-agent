FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything clients ask before working with Open Agent. Still unsure? Email or WhatsApp us directly.

What does Open Agent do?

Open Agent is an AI-powered digital marketing agency founded by Abeer Nasir. We run Meta Ads, build Shopify stores and websites, set up AI automation, generate leads, and manage social media for e-commerce brands and founders.

How much do Meta Ads cost?

Our management fee is separate from your ad spend. Ad budgets typically start from $300–$500/month depending on your market. We'll quote a flat fee after a free strategy call.

Do you work with clients outside Pakistan?

Yes. Our primary target markets are the US, UK, and Canada, alongside Pakistan. Everything is handled remotely, so location is not a barrier.

What results can I expect?

Results depend on budget, offer, and niche. We focus on ROAS, CPL, and CTR — not vanity metrics. Past creatives and campaigns are shown in our portfolio.

How long until we start?

Most projects begin within 3–5 days of our first call. Shopify and website builds may take 1–2 weeks depending on scope.

Do you use AI in your work?

Yes — AI is core to how we operate. We use AI for ad creatives, content systems, chatbots, and workflow automation to deliver more, faster, and leaner.
