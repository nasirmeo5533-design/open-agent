About Abeer Nasir

From curiosity to practical AI.

I didn't follow a fixed roadmap into AI. I learned by building — real projects, real clients, real problems. That's how I got good.

My story

Turning ideas into practical solutions

I started learning Generative AI in 2023 with one simple question: how can AI help businesses work better? I didn't have a traditional AI background and no fixed roadmap — so I learned by building. My first projects were rough, but each one taught me what actually works versus what just looks impressive.

The learning curve was real: I broke campaigns, wrote weak prompts, and built automations that didn't fit how a business actually runs. Every mistake sharpened the next build. By 2024 I was taking on real clients — and that's where it clicked: businesses don't want "AI for the sake of AI," they want less manual work and more sales.

Specific wins I'm proud of: turning a PKR 1,000 Meta Ads budget into 30 sales for a perfume D2C brand, generating 6 qualified leads from PKR 2,000 for a local service, and building a no-code follow-up system that gave a founder 2 hours back every day. I've also written two free ebooks — on Digital Marketing and Generative AI — to help other beginners skip the confusion I went through.

Today I help e-commerce and D2C brands use AI and digital marketing to improve workflows, strengthen their online presence, and grow with clear, measurable results.
