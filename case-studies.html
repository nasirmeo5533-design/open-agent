Small budgets, real numbers.

Meta Ads · E-commerce · Perfume/D2C

Small-budget Meta Ads test — perfume D2C brand

Niche: D2C perfume & beauty e-commerce.
Audience: Women 18–34 in Pakistan interested in fragrance & affordable luxury, tested via interest + broad targeting.
Creative angle: UGC-style product reels showing the scent/look, plus a “first-order discount” hook in the first 3 seconds.
Timeline: 10-day campaign, optimized daily; best creative scaled from day 4.
What I did: Tight audience testing, fast creative iteration, and daily bid/budget tuning on Meta Ads.
Result: 30 qualified sales from just PKR 1,000 of ad spend — roughly a 3x return on ad spend.

Lead Gen · Local Service

Lead-generation test — local service business

Niche: Local service business (appointment-based).
Audience: City-level prospects searching for the service, narrowed by intent signals.
Creative angle: A clear “book a free call” offer with a single strong CTA and a customer-result line.
Timeline: 2-week campaign; leads steady from day 2 after the first creative fix.
What I did: Focused campaign with strong offer and audience targeting on Meta, plus a instant WhatsApp follow-up link.
Result: 6 qualified leads from PKR 2,000 — proof small budgets work with right targeting.

Shopify · D2C Beauty

Store optimization for a perfume brand

Niche: Perfume/D2C beauty on Shopify.
Audience: Existing traffic converting poorly at checkout.
Creative angle: AI-assisted collection descriptions + scent-story copy that matched the ad message.
Timeline: 2-week store refresh, shipped in sprints.
What I did: Product-page cleanup, clearer CTAs, faster mobile checkout, and AI-assisted content for collections.
Result: Smoother buying experience and a stronger, on-brand storefront with consistent ad-to-page message.

AI Automation

Lead follow-up on autopilot

Niche: Service founder drowning in manual replies.
Audience: Incoming leads from ads + WhatsApp.
Creative angle: No-code workflow (LLM + form + WhatsApp) that sounds human, not robotic.
Timeline: Built & live in under a week.
What I did: Captured, tagged and followed up with leads automatically — instant acknowledgement + timed nudges.
Result: ~2 hours saved daily and faster response to every inquiry, so no lead went cold.
